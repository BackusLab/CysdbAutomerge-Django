How to Use CysDB Automerge

Follow these steps to upload your protein data and manage it effectively.

Uploading a CSV File

To upload a CSV file, navigate to the Upload Page and follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Choose File" button and select your CSV file.
  2. Click the "Upload" button to submit your file.
  3. Once the upload is complete, you will be navigated to different page to have an overview of the csv uploaded.
  4. You can then download your data, which will be saved as a .pdf file

Managing Your Data

After uploading, you can manage your data through the admin interface:

  1. Go to the Admin Page and log in with your credentials.
  2. Click on the "Cysdb" section to view and manage your protein entries.
  3. You can add, edit, or delete entries as needed.

Viewing Data

To view the data you have uploaded, you can:

  1. Navigate to the Admin Page to see an overview of all proteins uploaded.
  2. Use the search functionality to find specific proteins.
